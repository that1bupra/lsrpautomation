About

Rensselaer County Roleplay Unity has flourished as a vibrant and engaging community since its establishment on July 22, 2023. Dedicated to providing a diverse range of roleplaying experiences and opportunities, the server fosters creativity and collaboration among its members.

All participants are respectfully encouraged to review the regulations outlined below to ensure a welcoming, respectful, and enjoyable environment for everyone.

Server Rules

To ensure a great experience for everyone, please follow our community guidelines and rules.

Be Respectful

Always treat others with kindness and understanding. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bullying will not be tolerated under any circumstances. We're here to build a positive space - not tear each other down.

No NSFW/NSFL Content

This is a safe-for-work community, so keep things appropriate. Explicit, violent, or disturbing content is not allowed in any form. This includes text, images, videos, usernames, or profile pictures.

No Doxxing

Doxing - sharing someone's real name, address, contact info, or any personal data - is strictly forbidden. It is illegal, dangerous, and will result in a permanent ban. We take privacy violations very seriously.
